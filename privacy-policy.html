When you visit our custom rolling trays landing page, we may collect:

  • Device information and IP address
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process your custom rolling tray orders
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • MunchMakers.com (our parent company) for order fulfillment
  • Third-party analytics providers (Google, Facebook)
  • Payment processors for transaction processing
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For marketing communications and non-essential cookies
  • Contract: To fulfill custom rolling tray orders
  • Legitimate Interest: For website analytics and improving our services
  • Legal Obligation: For tax and accounting purposes

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for:

  • Order data: 7 years for tax and legal purposes
  • Marketing data: Until you withdraw consent
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Website logs: 12 months

6. Cookies and Tracking

This site uses cookies and tracking pixels to:

  • Track conversions and marketing performance
  • Provide personalized advertising
  • Analyze user behavior and preferences

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), if you are located in the EU, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Disable cookies in your browser

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
