Refund Policy

Last updated: September 7, 2025

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

  • Products received are defective or damaged
  • Order was incorrect due to our error
  • Products do not match the approved proof
  • Request is made within 30 days of receipt

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items cannot be refunded:

  • Custom products made to your specifications
  • Products damaged due to mishandling after delivery
  • Orders placed with incorrect shipping information
  • Digital proofs or design services

3. Defective Products

  • We will replace or refund defective products at no cost
  • Photo evidence of defects may be required
  • Return shipping will be covered by MunchMakers
  • Replacement products will be expedited

4. Refund Process

  1. Contact us within 30 days of receipt
  2. Provide order number and reason for refund
  3. Include photos if claiming defect or damage
  4. We will review and approve eligible refunds
  5. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

5. Shipping Costs

  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs are customer responsibility (unless defective)
  • Expedited shipping fees are not refundable

6. Proof Approval

  • Customers must approve digital proofs before production
  • Once proof is approved, design changes may incur additional costs
  • Refunds for approved designs are limited to production errors only

7. Payment Refunds

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Credit card refunds may take 3-5 business days to appear
  • PayPal refunds are typically immediate

8. Order Cancellations

  • Orders can be cancelled before production begins
  • Cancellation requests must be made within 24 hours
  • Once production starts, orders cannot be cancelled

9. Contact for Refunds

To request a refund, contact us:

Email: help@munchmakers.com
Phone: +1 650-640-3836
Subject: "Refund Request - Order #[Your Order Number]"

10. Resolution Timeline

  • Initial response within 24 hours
  • Refund decision within 3 business days
  • Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days
